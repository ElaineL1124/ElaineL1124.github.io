• Check your surroundings and weather conditions before performing a lift.
  • Check that shackles are correctly installed and secured.
  • Check rigging arrangements.
  • Keep yourself and others out of the hoisting area.
  • Beware of potential obstructions.
  • Check the wind: is it above 12 m/s wind and 18 m/s gusts, do not perform lift. Note: there may be lifts that involve more restrictive limits.
