The following preparations shall be done prior to the installation of first components:

·Position the containers in accordance with the corresponding component lift plan. Each component has a unique trailer position.

· Mark out the working area.

· Block off the lift zone using pylons all the way around the tower at a radius of 25m.

· Establish radio contact between the crane operator in the nacelle and the ground crew.

· Assemble and check rigging arrangements. Make sure that the orientation of the shackles is correct.

· Remove the container tarp and transport lashing of the trailers.
